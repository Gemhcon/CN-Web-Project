Halloumi Fries

Who need's chips when you have Halloumi Fries! Soft creamy cheese surrounded by a smokey crunchy outer layer is a great addition to any meal or simply as a snack. Best of all they are really easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 250g packs of halloumi chopped into fries
  • 75g Plain Flour
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • Sunflower Oil for deep frying

Method

  1. Mix the smoked paprika with the flour. Then coat the Halloumi with the flour smoked paprika mix.
  2. alf fill your pan with oil and heat to 190°C or until a piece of bread browns in 30 seconds. If it browns quicker it is too hot and you need to reduce the temp and try again .
  3. Once the oil is hot carefully add a few of the Halloumi fries at a time (don’t add all at the same time as this will reduced the temp of the oil).
  4. Fry the fries for 1 to 2 minutes (until the golden brown). Remove and drain on kitchen roll.

Serving Suggestion

The Halloumi fries go well with our Seafood Burger
