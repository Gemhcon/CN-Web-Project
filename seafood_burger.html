Seafood Burger

Get the kids eating more fish with this seafood burger recipe. It's packed with cod, prawns and crab meat and it is that easy to make you can even get the kids to help.

Ingredients

  • 1 x large cod fillet
  • 100g shelled and deveined king prawns
  • 100g Mix of white and brown crab meat
  • 4 Shallots finely chopped
  • 1/2 red chilli pepper finely sliced (optional)
  • 1 Sunflower Oil
  • 4 slices of Chorizo
  • 2 Seeded Brioche Buns
  • Chopped avocado
  • Baby gem lettuce

Method

  1. Fry the shallots and red chilli in a 2 tsp of sunflower oil until soft. Then set aside to cool.
  2. Chop the cod into small pieces and place in a food processor and blitz for 10 to 20 seconds.
  3. Finely sliced the prawns into small pieces.
  4. Add the crab meat (breaking up any larger pieces) sliced prawns, cod and cooled shallot and red chilli together. Season and mix well.
  5. Split the mixture into two, and form two burger shaped patties. Cover with cling film and place in the fridge for at least an hour.
  6. When ready to cook heat 2 tbs of sunflower oil in a non stick pan and fry for 3 minutes on each side until cooked through.
  7. emove burgers from pan, wipe out and fry chorizo slices for around a minute turning half way through.
  8. Toast your brioche buns until slightly brown, add the avocado, lettuce leaves, burger and chorizo to the bun.

Serving Suggestion

The Seafood Burger goes well with our easy Halloumi Fries
